About
DamStab performs preliminary stability screening of concrete gravity dams per FERC Chapter 3 and USACE EM 1110-2-2100.
Checks include: sliding FS, overturning (Mr/Mo), eccentricity, resultant location, and base pressures.
Load cases: Usual (normal pool), Flood, Seismic, Extreme (seismic + flood).
This tool is for screening only. A licensed professional engineer must verify all results.
